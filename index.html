INTRODUCTION

Pedro Pascal is a Chilean-American actor who has gained widespread recognition for his work in film and television. Born on April 2, 1975, in Santiago, Chile, Pascal spent much of his childhood traveling with his parents, who were both involved in the arts. His mother was a singer and child psychologist, while his father was a fertility doctor and political activist.
